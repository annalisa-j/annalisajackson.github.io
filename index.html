Meet your coach

Hi, I'm Annalisa

I'm a somatic dating and relationship coach committed to helping you build flourishing relationships. My work is all about helping you deeply know yourself and come to trust your body's intuition when it comes to love and connection. As an eldest daughter, and someone who grew up living between two very different cultural contexts, I got used to shape-shifting into whatever my environment needed me to be. These skills helped me excel in my job (I worked in peace building and conflict resolution for many years) but they also led to minimizing my needs which manifested as chronic stress and health issues. This forced me to take a closer look at what my own healing could really look like.

Discovering somatic coaching sent me on a journey of coming home to my own body, healing the parts that were disconnected and living into more boldness, courage and authenticity to be more of who I want to be. What started as a health journey, completely shifted how I showed up in my romantic relationships. I went from over-giving and quieting my intuition, to honoring my needs, trusting myself more confidently and saying "yes" to what truly felt aligned for me in love.

Since completing my certification in Somatic Coaching, I've had the honor of walking alongside 350+ incredible humans on their path to more embodied confidence, connection to self and flourishing relationships. To me this work is also spiritual — it's about connecting to where we come from, to mystery, and to the depths of ourselves that are longing to be known. What I want for you is a tender relationship to your inner wisdom, a kindness to yourself, a clarity in what you stand for and a love that blows you away — starting with the one you have with yourself.

Questions you might have

Somatic coaching is an approach to personal transformation that acknowledges we can't just think our way to new behaviors—we have to practice new ways of being. We combine talking about the challenges you're facing with learning somatic practices to support you in the areas you want to grow: staying calm under pressure, setting boundaries, leading with confidence in your relationships.

While we create space to honor your past, our work is focused forward. We focus on supporting you to move towards your goals in dating and relationships while paying attention to where old embodied habits might be getting in the way. Many of my clients are also in therapy and find that somatic coaching is the missing link between insight and lasting change.

This program is for women who are dating or in relationships and want to show up with more confidence, clarity, and self-trust. Whether you're navigating the uncertainty of dating, working through challenges in a current relationship, or building your sense of self before entering the next one—this community will meet you where you are.
